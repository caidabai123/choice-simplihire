Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from employers

A

We specialize in recruiting for a wide range of positions including software developers, data analysts, product managers, designers, marketing specialists, accounting/finance professionals, and more. Our talent pool covers various industries including technology, finance, healthcare, and professional services.

A

We provide recruitment services across 7 major developed countries: United States, United Kingdom, Ireland, Australia, New Zealand, Canada, and Singapore. We have established local networks and expertise in each of these markets.

A

We offer flexible fee structures tailored to your needs. Our standard model is a success-based fee, where you only pay when we successfully place a candidate. A
